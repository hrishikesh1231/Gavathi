# 🥭 GAVATHI – Konkan Marketplace Platform

## 🚀 Overview

GAVATHI is a startup-oriented MERN Stack marketplace designed to connect customers from major cities with authentic local products from the Konkan region.

Unlike traditional e-commerce platforms, GAVATHI follows a **Request → Seller Response → Customer Confirmation** workflow that helps prevent inventory mismatches, delivery issues, and order cancellations.

The platform currently serves customers across multiple cities while empowering local Konkan sellers to sell regional products online.

---

# 💡 Problem Statement

Traditional marketplaces often suffer from:

* Stock availability uncertainty
* Delivery feasibility issues
* Product customization limitations
* Order cancellations after purchase
* Difficulty for small local sellers to manage inventory

For local Konkan businesses, these problems become even bigger because many products are seasonal, handmade, or available in limited quantities.

---

# ✅ Our Solution

GAVATHI introduces a unique order validation workflow:

### Step 1

Customer submits an order request.

### Step 2

Seller reviews:

* Product availability
* Requested quantity
* Delivery location
* Packaging requirements
* Estimated delivery timeline

### Step 3

Seller responds within 24 hours with:

* Final price
* Available quantity
* Discount (if applicable)
* Delivery charges
* Expected delivery date

### Step 4

Customer reviews seller response.

### Step 5

Customer confirms order using Cash On Delivery (COD).

### Step 6

Order enters fulfillment workflow.

This reduces fake orders, stock conflicts, and delivery failures.
